# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We aim to keep this project focused, respectful, and useful for open-source research and engineering work. Contributors and maintainers are expected to communicate professionally and assume good faith while discussing code, product behavior, generated assets, and documentation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in issues, pull requests, and reviews.
- Keep discussions technical and evidence-based.
- Share enough context for others to reproduce or understand your report.
- Respect data, privacy, licensing, quota, and generated-asset boundaries.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private data, credentials, user uploads, or non-redistributable artifacts.
- Misrepresenting generated-content quality, deployment state, or provenance.
- Repeated off-topic or disruptive comments after maintainer guidance.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; reject pull requests; or block users when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
